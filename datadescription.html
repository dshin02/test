Description of Transportation Data

This page provides detailed descriptions of the data used in the visualizations, such as sources, methods, and key insights.

Detailed explanation of the bar graph and line graph

  • Car Usage: Information on vehicle ownership and usage in Indiana.
  • Public Transportation Usage: Data on bus and train services.
  • Bicycle Use: Trends in cycling as a mode of transportation.
  • Taxi, Motorcycle, or other: Information on people who uses taxi, motorcycle, or other to commute
  • Worked at Home: Data on people who work at home and don't need to commute
  • Walked: Trends on people that commute by walking instead of transportation
